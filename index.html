Parking

City of Ferndale Parking Information

If you're coming out for just the toast and after-party, you're in luck: parking enforcement ends at 9pm. Otherwise, there are several paid lots in the area.

Even better: parking is free on Sundays. That means you can come back in the morning to retrieve your car, if you had to leave it behind.

Please, do not find out the hard way that parking in Ferndale neighborhoods usually gets you a parking ticket. Pay a few dollars ($0.50/hour) and park in a lot. If you're too cheap for that, there is a free parking lot at the American Legion (corner of 9Mile and Livernois).

Drunk Driving

Ready for this? Don't do it.

The majority of our friends - whether it be insanity, stupidity, or thrill-seeking - have a terrible track record at drunk driving. If you're coming to celebrate our wedding, please don't drive home drunk. You're not only putting yourself/others in danger, but you're sh*tting on our wishes. If we find out you drove home drunk, we swear we'll never talk to you again.

There is a 99% chance we're going to hire a big van to take us home. Please, get on the van with us and let us pay to drive your drunk ass home.

Protip: being Irish, Polish, or Martian does not make you invulnerable from getting drunk and receiving a DUI (or crashing).
